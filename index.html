The Adventures of an Long Island-Italian in Rome!


I traveled to Rome, Italy for my project. I had an amazing time with my friends and classmates! Interact with my map to follow along with my adventures!


I did not what to expect traveling abroad for the first time, I just knew that it was a necessary thing for me to do at the time. I have always wanted to travel and this class and program gave me the opportunity and I am super grateful for that! Even though I had a rocky start with having a 2 hour long anxiety attack in the airport and on the plane, I was put at ease when we landed in Rome, Italy. Traveling abroad has been on my mind since I was a kid, and my cousin has shown me how incredible it is to travel! She had a plan to travel to 30 countries before she turned 30, and she did indeed accomplish that goal! She inspires me to want to become a well-traveled indiviudal like herself! I had my sights set on Italy because I do come from an Italian family and I wanted to experience the culture firsthand in the country of origin opposed to my grandma's house XD (no offense to grandma of course). In 2019, I had signed up to study abroad in Rome for the entire Spring Semester, but due to Ms. Rona I was unable to go considering at the time Rome was a major hot-spot for the virus. So when there was another opportunity to travel to Rome, I could not pass it up!
